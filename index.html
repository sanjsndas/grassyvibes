Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn maintenance?
We recommend weekly maintenance during growing season (spring and summer) and bi-weekly during slower growth periods. Our team will create a customized schedule based on your lawn's specific needs and grass type.
Do you provide free estimates?
Yes! We offer complimentary consultations and estimates for all our services. Our team will visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.
What makes your service eco-friendly?
We prioritize native Texas plants, water-efficient irrigation systems, organic fertilizers, and integrated pest management. Our sustainable practices reduce environmental impact while maintaining beautiful landscapes.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely! GrassyVibesAtHome is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation for your peace of mind.
